About Ionical Inc

Ionical Inc is a Hong Kong-based holding company specializing in comfort-focused apparel brands. We oversee operations across multiple subsidiaries in Australia, the United States, and the Netherlands, serving as the strategic hub for our growing portfolio of specialized comfort wear brands.

Our investment strategy focuses on brands that redefine comfort in fashion, from everyday essentials to luxury loungewear, active performance wear, and therapeutic comfort solutions.
